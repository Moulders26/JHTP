Due to the nature of natural stone, no two pieces may be exactly alike and we can not predict any varying colour differences. Natural quarried materials may vary slightly in colour, shade and grain size. Light grey granites and white granites tend to be more absorbent than darker granites, which could lead to staining by objects placed on the memorials such as flowers, wreaths or rust etc.

We work closely with China when designing and processing your memorial. Everything is carefully crafted by hand to ensure a truly unique memorial.
